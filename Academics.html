The Bob Jones Curriculum

Is a curriculum built around the Christian worldview. Its core values are based on biblical teachings. The curriculum is based on providing children with reading materials that enable critical thinking and practical learning. It has courses for children in K3 up to the 12th-grade level. Subjects offered are Math, English, Science, Heritage Studies, Reading, Spelling, Shona, Music, Physical Education and Bible.Bob Jones Curriculum has brought technology into classrooms which has had a positive impact on the quality of education delivered. The students use eBooks which ensure that learners are proactively interacting with the learning material by way of videos, animations, augmented reality, changing displays, and taking notes.We believe education should be shaped according to the lens of Scripture. A complete education goes beyond facts and processes, so our textbooks and eBooks direct students to think for themselves. We teach learners to live life with a Godly purpose and thus set and pursue goals that thrust them towards their God-given purpose.

Infants Departments

K3 to Grade 2

The Infants Department at CIS caters for students aged 3 to 9 years in age. Our K3 class is the main entry point into the school and students are welcomed to their CIS learning adventure by Miss Ruwona and her assistant “Ma B”. 

Our 4- to 5-year-olds are set up for success in our K4 class with Miss Tapson and Miss Cynthia. They then move on to K5, which is the equivalent of Grade 1. Here Mrs. Soko and Mrs. Samusodza develop those basic skills of reading, writing and arithmetic. Our 6- to -7-year-old students have a wonderful time in Grade 1 with Miss Gwenzi and Miss Julias. The leaders in our department are the grade 2’s who are taught by Miss Jackson and Mrs. Mushonga. In the second grade the children learn how to do beautiful penmanship using cursive script. All our classrooms grow to no more than 20 students per class and each room provides a unique environment for the students. We have everything from a jungle - complete with a swing, to a lofty loft and cozy cave!

Our main aim is to expose children to the power of God’s love, His Word and His character. By doing this we provide a strong Biblical foundation for their faith to develop upon. Our sports program includes gymnastics, athletics and swimming.

Critical thinking is a skill that is practiced daily, as we desire for the next generation of Zimbabweans to be problem solvers. As a department we would love to meet you, and introduce your child to Celebration International Infants Department!

Elementary departments

Grade2 to Grade 6.

Grade2 to Grade 6. Celebration International School (CIS) provides a learning culture that facilitates development of learners’ attitudes, beliefs and skills needed for success. Leaners are challenged with tasks that involve developing effective communication skills: oral, written, and digital. We prepare students to be life-long learners with strong character using the Bob Jones University (BJU) curriculum.  The Bob Jones Curriculum is a curriculum built around the Christian worldview. Its core values are based on biblical teachings. The curriculum is based on providing children with reading materials that enable critical thinking and practical learning. It has courses for children in K3 up to the 12th-grade level. Subjects offered are Math, English, Science, Heritage Studies, Reading, Spelling, Shona, Music, Physical Education and Bible.  Bob Jones Curriculum has brought technology into classrooms which has had a positive impact on the quality of education delivered. The students use eBooks which ensure that learners are proactively interacting with the learning material by way of videos, animations, augmented reality, changing displays, and taking notes. We believe education should be shaped according to the lens of Scripture. A complete education goes beyond facts and processes, so our textbooks and eBooks direct students to think for themselves. We teach learners to live life with a Godly purpose and thus set and pursue goals that thrust them towards their God- given purpose.

High School Department

Our curriculum places a great emphasis on teamwork, problem solving and learners get to experience a Biblical and a highly academic curriculum which prepares them for the Kingdom of God and the marketplace. Our dedicated teachers, well-rounded approach and enhanced curriculum empower learners with 21st century learning skills through subjects like Robotics and use of effective teaching methods.
