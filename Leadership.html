Principal Birditt

WELCOME to the Celebration International School website.  Here, you will find answers to questions you have about the unique learning experiences we offer children aged four to eighteen.  What sets us apart from other schools in Zimbabwe is that our curriculum is Christian.  Currently, we use an American Christian curriculum published by Bob Jones University in South Carolina.  For the time being, we also offer Cambridge IGCSE and A level. 

Now, more than ever, children need to learn the truth that is in God's Word, the Bible.  A biblical world-view in education gives children the confidence to know who God is and who God intended them to be in Jesus Christ.  This is not achieved at the expense of academic rigour as our Christian curriculum promotes creativity, critical thinking and collaboration. We are preparing young people for life, not just for public exams. 

Our job is Christian Education, but the business we are in is strengthening families.  Whoever you are and wherever you are in the world, you will have witnessed just how much families are under threat.  Families are being weakened by addictions, separation and economic woes.  If we allow families to be destroyed we run the risk of anarchy in the world. 

Thanks and regards
GB

Melanie du Toit - H.O.D 
Middle School

With over three decades of experience as an educator, my focus lies in classroom management and curriculum development. My passion for Christian education drives me to create an environment where students thrive academically and spiritually. By fostering inclusive relationships with students, parents, and colleagues, I aim to build a collaborative and supportive educational community. Guided by a belief in the inherent worth of young individuals, my mission is to instil compassion, integrity, and servant leadership shaping responsible citizens and ambassadors for the Kingdom and the marketplace.

Mrs Munyaradzi - H.O.D
 High School

I am privileged to be a member of the team that transforms lives and reforms nations.
